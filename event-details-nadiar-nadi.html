Seminar on "Nadiar Nadi"

This informative event held on 6th July 2024 at Debagram S A Vidyapith Seminar Hall drew significant participation from alumni and local residents. The session featured an enlightening lecture and discussion on the river dynamics of Nadia, presented by:

  • Mr. Supratim Karmakar: Renowned River Expert and Columnist.

The engaging session provided valuable insights into the history, challenges, and conservation efforts related to the rivers in the Nadia district. Attendees had the opportunity to interact with the speaker during the Q&A session, gaining deeper knowledge and perspectives on the subject.
