Lecture and Interactive Session on Education and Career

This engaging event held on 10th August 2024 at Debagram S A Vidyapith Seminar Hall was attended by many alumni and current students. The session featured insightful talks on educational pathways and career opportunities delivered by:

  • Mr. Arjun Sarathi Das: Asst. Professor of Economics, Savitri Girls College, Kolkata.
  • Mr. Ram Krishna Jaiswal: Hon. Consul, Maldives.

The interactive session included a Q&A portion where attendees had the opportunity to engage directly with the speakers, gaining valuable advice and guidance.
