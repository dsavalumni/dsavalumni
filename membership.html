Join the Alumni Association

Becoming a member of the Debagram Shibram Anandamayee Vidyapith Alumni Association allows you to reconnect with old friends, contribute to meaningful initiatives, and stay updated on school events and activities. Choose a membership option that best suits you:

Membership Options

Membership Type Fee (INR) Benefits
1-Year Membership Rs 100/- Access to alumni events and regular updates.
Lifetime Membership Rs 1000/- Permanent membership with lifelong access to all alumni events.
Gold Membership Rs 2500/- Includes lifetime membership, 10th reunion registration (for you and your spouse), space in the souvenir to share memories, and a free pass for the classical music night program.
